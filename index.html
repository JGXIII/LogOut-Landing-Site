The full walkthrough

Two ways to lock

You choose when the

Delay Lock

Scroll freely while the countdown runs. The moment it hits zero, your distracting apps lock automatically. Perfect for “ten more minutes, then I'm done” — except this time it's actually ten.

Instant Lock

Everything distracting locks the second your session starts, so there's nothing to reach for while you focus, study, or sleep. The walk still stands between you and the feed afterwards.
