1. Use of the App

LogOut helps you build healthier phone habits by locking distracting apps and requiring walking steps to restore access. The App offers two modes:

  • Standard mode — a session timer runs in the background; app blocking activates when the timer ends, and walking the required steps restores access
  • Focus Lock mode — selected app categories are blocked immediately when the session starts. Blocking is enforced by a background system extension that remains active even if the App is closed. An emergency exit is available during the countdown timer for genuine emergencies; using it skips the remaining timer and advances directly to the walk-to-unlock step

App categories blocked during a session are configurable via the Focus Rules screen. Blocking is powered by Apple's Screen Time framework and applies system-wide.
